About

SPINBIN is a project by Alastair Porter and J Herskowitz. It creates auto-updating playlists out of webpages.

You can add any playlist directly to Tomahawk (or any other player that supports them). When the page updates, the playlist updates, and Tomahawk updates. Magic.... ish.


Get Tomahawk

Request Playlist Addition

Know a site that has a good list of tracks - or, even better - a page where new tracks are added regularly? Best new tracks sites, radio playlist pages, playlists on other music services? Just tweet at us @spinb_in and we will work on adding it.
